Embedded Firmware

Firmware is a piece of code that is programmed into the hardware. The firmware is integrated at the hardware level, term firm literally means something which is firm and can’t be changed easily, unlike ‘software. Embedded firmware has become a critical part of today’s computing requirement due to a wide of gadgets. We at Maram Technologies have an in house team of highly skilled and experienced embedded engineers to help program based on your needs and requirements.

Android Open Source Project (AOSP)

AOSP is an open source version of android made available by Google. Android Open source Projects gives developer the ability and liberty to modify, customize and tweak the android operating system as per their needs and requirements. With the advent of Internet of Things (IoT), AOSP has made it possible for developers to create custom variants of the Android OS, port devices and accessories to the Android platform, and ensure devices meet the compatibility requirements that keep the Android ecosystem a healthy and stable environment for millions of users.

Mobile Device Management (MDM)

It is required that the system (android media player) operate in a controlled environment. Administrator should be able to remotely monitor the health of the network of devices and take corrective action if needed. Also, it is important for administrator to provision only Apps which are relevant and needed for the solution. The platform also allows updating current apps over the air and installation of new applications remotely in the future in case required to extend the capability and features

How It Works

⮚ Each device enrolls with the server and downloads the configuration file.
⮚ As per the configuration file, the system downloads required applications and settings.
⮚ The IOT agent performs required actions such as app installations, settings etc.
⮚ Devices periodically updates the monitoring parameters to the server.
